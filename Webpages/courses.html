Learning Center

Enhance your cybersecurity knowledge with our comprehensive educational resources. From fundamentals to advanced practices, learn how to protect yourself and your organization.

Level 1

The Essentials

Focus: Fundamental digital hygiene, recognizing primary entry points for fraud, and basic protection.
Key Topics:
  • Phishing
  • Strong Passwords
  • MFA
Level 2

Social & Financial Safety

Focus: Protecting financial transactions, social media interactions, and personal identity.
Key Topics:
  • UPI/QR scams
  • Social Media Privacy
  • Public Wi-Fi traps
Level 3

Modern AI & Technical Threats

Focus: Advanced technology-based scams, malicious software, and device security.
Key Topics:
  • Deepfakes
  • Voice Cloning
  • Malicious App Permissions
Level 4

Corporate & Community Responsibility

Focus: Workplace safety, protecting official identity, and the legal reporting process.
Key Topics:
  • Tailgating (Physical Security)
  • Software Updates
  • Reporting Cybercrime
